ABOUT BRACELETIQUE

Staying true to the brand’s belief that everyone deserves to shine,
each piece of jewellery is meaningfully conceptualised and beautifully crafted.

BRACELETIQUE was founded in 2013 with the concept that the beauty, elegance, and charm of high-quality jewels should be accessible to all modern women. BRACELETIQUE immediately became renowned as the modern jewellery famed for its high quality and stunningly beautiful diamond jewellery, putting a strong focus on innovation and design brilliance.

Necklace

From delicate chains to statement pendants, our collection of necklaces offers something for everyone. Whether you prefer classic and timeless designs or bold and trendy pieces, we
have a necklace that will suit your taste.

Bracelet

Our collection of bracelets includes a wide range of styles, from classic and elegant to modern and edgy. We offer bangles, cuffs, charm bracelets, and more, each designed to make a statement and elevate your look.

Creativity

From inspiration brief to collection launch, Swarovski’s creative process is uniquely prolific. With a focus on trend curation, design, and meaningful branding, every piece tells a story and embodies over 125 years of mastered craftsmanship. The continuous evolution of material and technique makes Swarovski the leader of crystal cut creations.


Know-How

New cuts, shades, and sizes are conceived and realized every day in the historical Wattens headquarters, pushing the boundaries of crystal. Swarovski’s know-how not only focuses on refining the crystal, it also explores methods that make the most incredible pieces possible. Beading, pavé, bezel, and prong settings are some of the techniques used to enhance the sparkle and create exquisite refinement.

Quality

BRACELETIQUE crystal settings come to life through our proprietary techniques such as Pointiage®, which lend an ultra-precise quality to work done by hand. Our attention to detail ensures that each and every piece will always be made of the highest quality. This expertise is infused in every step of the creative process, from initial design sketches through assembly.
