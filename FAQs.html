General Information

Braceletique offers a full statutory warranty covering manufacturing and material defects for genuine branded braceletique products purchased from Braceletique's own stores (including Online) or authorized Swarovski retailers. The warranty only covers damage that, in the opinion of Braceletique or an authorized Braceletique retailer, is not caused by accident or inappropriate use, ordinary wear and tear, modification, attempted repair, negligence, or by failure to follow Braceletique's Jewelry Care Advice.

We work hard to process your order and get your purchases out for delivery quickly and efficiently. Order cancellation may be possible within 30 minutes (approximately) of receipt of your order confirmation. If cancellation is no longer possible, unwanted items can be returned per our returns policy. Alternatively, the parcel can also be refused upon arrival and our delivery partner will return the parcel to our warehouse, with no extra charges for you.

Order Process

All information and new arrivals can find out on our Home page. Follow up BRACELETIQUE official website and social account to get the first notification.

For security reasons, BRACELETIQUE Online does not save credit card information. The data is immediately passed on to the company's partner systems for payment processing. If you believe that someone has tried to misuse your credit card, please follow the instructions of your credit card issuer immediately and contact Customer Care.

The details provided to BRACELETIQUE will not be forwarded to any third parties under any circumstances.

Shipping

Standard Delivery - City-Link Express

Orders placed from Monday to Friday by 10:00 AM will be processed and shipped the same business day.
Standard delivery time: 2-7 business days after processing and shipping

Klang Valley: 2-3 business days after processing and shipping

Peninsular: 3-5 business days after processing and shipping

Sabah: 5-7 business days after processing and shipping

Sarawak: 4-6 business days after processing and shipping

Free standard shipping for all orders with no minimum spend.

Orders placed on weekends and national holidays will be processed and shipped two business days later.

It is not always possible to change the delivery address or delivery date once the parcel is in transit. You might have the possibility to organize the delivery of your parcel in the most suitable way for you, by utilizing our delivery partners' web portal.

Delivery to another country/region is not possible. Please place the order in the online shop of the country/region where the parcel is being delivered to.

Please check the shipping confirmation email to track your parcel

If the tracking shows that your parcel has been delivered, please check if someone else at your address has accepted it, alternatively, your parcel may have been left with a neighbour.
